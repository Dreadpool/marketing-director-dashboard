# Product Mission

## Problem

Marketing directors manage dozens of recurring workflows each month, quarter, and year. These workflows are analysis-heavy, requiring data from multiple platforms (ad networks, analytics, sales databases, SEO tools, google sheets each one requiring a different login and interface and google sheets that change structure), each with its own interface and data format. Directors waste time context-switching between tools, manually pulling data, and trying to remember what needs to be done and when. Strategic decisions get delayed because assembling the full picture takes too long.

## Target Users

Marketing directors managing multi-channel paid and organic marketing programs. Built first for Salt Lake Express (SLE) marketing operations, with the architecture designed to support other organizations in the future as a SaaS product.

## Solution

A workflow orchestration dashboard that surfaces the right marketing tasks at the right time, pre-loaded with the right data and AI context. The key differentiator is **workflow orchestration**: rather than being another dashboard or BI tool, the product knows *what* analysis needs to happen *when*, pulls the relevant data automatically, and provides a Claude-powered AI assistant pre-loaded with workflow-specific context (analysis frameworks, visualization requirements, action item guidelines, available data sources).

Each workflow pulls data from its relevant sources, visualizes it consistently, and lets the director make decisions and log action items before moving to the next task. A high-level dashboard on the main screen shows the core metrics the director is responsible for at a glance.
